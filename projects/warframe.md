I spent 4 months at Digital Extremes (May-August 2024) working as a C++ Gameplay Programmer on **Warframe**, a long-running third-person shooter/ninja action game with more than 80 million players. Play as Tenno and explore the Origin System while cutting through millions of enemies like butter. 

I helped develop new boss abilities for the [Technocyte Coda](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Technocyte_Coda) in the major [**Warframe: 1999**](https://www.warframe.com/en/1999) update, as well as fixing bugs across the game, including a bug with the game's engine that affected all raycast weapons in the game, and fixing several softlock bugs that could affect players if a host transition happened during a scripted event.

The game uses a custom C++ game engine, alongside Lua scripting.
