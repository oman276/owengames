*to progress, you must fail.*

*in this 2D platformer where platforms and obstacles only appear when you interact with them, you must step bravely into an unknown world to advance. you will hit dead ends, fall into pits, and get crushed. but with each death, the map fills out. your understanding of the game- and yourself- grows with each respawn. slowly, you build your own world.*

This game was made for the Wowie Jam 3.0, and released on March 1st, 2021. All art, design, and code was created by me. The music is from the free Wowie Asset Pack
