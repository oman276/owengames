I spent 8 months at Haven Studios (January-April 2025, and September-December 2025) working as a C++/Unreal Engine 5 Gameplay Programmer on **Fairgames**, an upcoming first-party PlayStation heist game launching on PS5 and PC. 

I focused on developing systems for narrative immersion in a heavily networked online game, including designing and implementing new data-driven systems for dialogue, animations, and cinematics, as well as more robust systems surrounding item interactions, game mode systems, and ensuring all UI was mouse-and-keyboard compatible for the PC audience.
