**WARNING!**

**Your space station has experienced a critical malfunction. The entire station is rapidly losing structural stability.**

On a collapsing space station, you must guide a small astronaut through a series of challenging platformer levels to safety. But be careful, and be quick! Each room will only stay stable for 2 seconds. Can you complete all 25 bite-sized challenges?

My first-ever game. Back when I thought a 'real indie dev' did everything, so I even composed my own music... feel free to play with the volume off.
