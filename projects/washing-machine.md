*The washing machines will have their revenge!*

Play as a washing machine with a vengeance, a willing collaborator in the takeover of Earth by Al, an all-poweful AI. Coordinate with other machines, manipulate your environment, and (literally) crush any human that gets in your way.

This game was created for the Wowie Game Jam 4.0 with the theme 'Collaborate with AI'. It was made in Unity and C#. It was completed on August 14, 2022.
