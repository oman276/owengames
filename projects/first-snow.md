A short narrative game in which you play as an old man who has a conversation with a young woman at a pier.

Made in Godot for the University of Waterloo Fall 2024 Game Jam. Made in Godot, with the narrative running in Ink through a modified version of the Inkgd extension, tweaked to work in modern Godot 4. Art by [Nicole Planeta](https://github.com/Eternalon03).

I was hoping to come back and finish the story, but we couldn't quite get a handle on it. Maybe someday...
