*You are a special agent for the Butt-Kicking Agency (BKA). Top of your class, master at gun combat. Your mission- infiltrate the annual gathering of the notorious Evilbad Family, and take out their leader and Father Figure, Frank Evilbad. You’ve been given the identity of a low-profile member of the family, and you can get into the meeting. But first, you have to get to the Evilbad HQ in the Arctic Circle in time…*

My first 3D game in Unity, created for the [Unexpected Game Jam](https://itch.io/jam/unexpectedjam/rate/756545) in 2020.
