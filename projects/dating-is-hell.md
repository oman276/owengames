*Hell isn't the only thing that's smouldering...*

You awake to find yourself in Dating Hell. Bargain with the Dating Devil to save your immortal soul, and reconnect with lost lovers you were far from close to...

This game was created on October 2, 2022 for the [University of Waterloo's Games Institute Jam](https://itch.io/jam/gi-game-jam-f22-university-of-waterloo). The jam was 48 hours, and the theme was "Far From Close".

Writing and programming by Owen Gallagher.  Art by Daegeon Kang.
