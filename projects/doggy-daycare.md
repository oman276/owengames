Welcome to your first day as supervisor at Doggy Daycare!

Can you take care of a group of increasingly rowdy dogs for 3 days? You need to manage giving them treats and pets, and be sure not to let them smash your priceless vases! Good Luck!

This game was made in Unity for the Extra Credits 2020 game jam.
