*Welcome, Recruit, to the Manifest Corporation! We are the leading bioscience research facility in the world. Ready to discover the future with us?*

An elevator accident leads to to crash into an abandoned sub-basement of the massive Manifest Corporation facility. Why was this portion sealed away? What lurks in the shadows? Can you escape with your life?

This game was made for the Black and White Jam 2021, in Category B, Greyscale. All art and code was created by me. Some sound effects are from freesound.org, and the music is from the Wowie Jam 3.0 Resources Pack
