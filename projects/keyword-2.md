I spent 4 months at City From Naught, Inc. (September-December 2024) working as a Unity/C# Gameplay Programmer on **Keyword 2: Nightfall**, an upcoming first-person detective game where you explore a cyberpunk city to uncover a new mystery.

I developed new Unity Editor tools to assist in optimization, helping automatically create low-detail models and implement the imposter technique for objects at high LOD, ensuring the game stays optimized in high-density, urban environments. I also integrated the FMOD audio tool into the game.

The game was developed in Unity and C#.
