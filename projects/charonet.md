A narrative game using Twine where you interact with an AI who is trying to determine whether you should go to heaven or hell.

*Hint: until you see the credits, the game isn't over. Keep looking.*

Created for [ENGL 294 - Intro to Critical Game Studies](https://uwflow.com/course/engl294) at the University of Waterloo. This was a project worked on with Liam MacArthur, Sophie Thomas-Salvosa, and Tanishi Naik.

Some choice comments from the itch.io page:

> i have cried no less than three times from this game now, one just reading caps as my friend played it. razor sharp writing.

> this game put me in an emotional rototiller and i came out as spaghetti. it's fantastic and gutting and beautiful.
