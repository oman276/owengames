Win the love of your life by plucking more roses than your opponent - or throw carrots at them to stop them from stealing your love from right under you! In this daring quest for love, only one shall prevail. Which dashing farmer will come out of this with a lover for life? The one that has bashed their opponent's skulls with carrots and has the most smelly red flowers to give of course.

A competitive local multiplayer game about winning the love of a beautiful tractor. Made with [Nicole Planeta](https://github.com/Eternalon03) in Unity and C#.
